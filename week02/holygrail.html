A Holy Grail Layout Example

Article 1

The Holy Grail is something of legend. It is believed that it was a cup that Christ had drank from and contained celestial power to heal those who drank from it.

Article 2

Many believe that King Arthur and his knights of the round table were in search of the Holy Grail. There have been many instances in history that may have been correlated to the search or use of the Grail. Such instance could be referred to the holy crusades of the past.
